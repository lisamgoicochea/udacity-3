Hamster

Hamsters can store food in their cheeks, then eat it later!

  • Scientific Name:Cricetinae
  • Average Length:1.8 - 5 inches
  • Average Lifespan:2 - 3 years
  • Habitat:Cage or wild

Chunky-bodied, thick-furred, short-tailed rodents with cheek pouches and are secretive burrowers and hoarders. Being nocturnal creatures, they usually sleep during the day and play and explore at night.
